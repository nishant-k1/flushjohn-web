## üìã **Step-by-Step: Using Virtual Address**

### **Step 1: Choose Service**

1. Research virtual address services
2. Compare prices and features
3. Choose one that:
   - Has Delaware address (or your preferred state)
   - Offers mail forwarding/scanning
   - Has good reviews
   - Fits your budget

### **Step 2: Sign Up**

1. Create account
2. Choose address location
3. Complete identity verification
4. **Complete USPS Form 1583** (required - see `USPS_FORM_1583_GUIDE.md`)
5. Pay monthly fee
6. Get your virtual address

### **Step 3: Complete USPS Form 1583 (REQUIRED)**

**‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL:** You must complete USPS Form 1583 for the service to receive mail!

1. **Download Form 1583** (from USPS or service)
2. **Fill out the form** (your info + service info)
3. **Get it notarized** (find notary in India - banks/law firms)
4. **Scan and submit** to your virtual address service
5. **Submit ID copy** (passport or driver's license)
6. **Wait for approval** (1-3 business days)

**üìñ Detailed guide:** See `USPS_FORM_1583_GUIDE.md` for complete instructions

**Once approved:**
- Service can receive mail
- Address becomes active
- Can receive GBP verification postcard

### **Step 4: Update GBP**

1. Go to GBP dashboard
2. Edit business information
3. Update address to virtual address
4. Save changes

### **Step 5: Request Verification**

1. Click "Get verified" in GBP
2. Choose postcard method
3. Postcard goes to virtual address
4. Service receives it
5. They notify you (email/app)
6. You get the code
7. Enter code in GBP dashboard
