## üí° Pro Tips

### 1. Use "URL Contains" for Multiple Pages

Instead of creating separate audiences for each city:
- Create one: "City Pages Visitors"
- Use: URL contains `/cities/` or `/city/`
- This captures all city pages in one audience

### 2. Exclude Converters (Advanced)

Once you have converters:
- Create "Quote Page Visitors (Non-Converters)"
- Include: URL contains `/quote`
- Exclude: People who converted

### 3. Combine Audiences (Advanced)

Create high-intent combinations:
- "Visited Quote OR Contact Page"
- "Visited Products AND City Page"
- "Blog Visitors who also visited Quote Page"
