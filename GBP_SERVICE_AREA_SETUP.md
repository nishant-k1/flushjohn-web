# How to Enable "Service-area business" and "No customer visits" in Google Business Profile

## üìç **Step-by-Step Instructions**

### **Step 1: Access Your GBP Dashboard**

1. Go to **https://business.google.com**
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select your **FlushJohn** business profile

---

### **Step 2: Enable Service-Area Business**

1. Click **"Edit profile"** (or the pencil icon)
2. Scroll down to find **"Service area"** or **"Service areas"** section
3. Look for a toggle or checkbox that says:
   - **"Service-area business"** or
   - **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"** or
   - **"This business serves customers at their location"**
4. **Enable/Check this option**

---

### **Step 3: Enable "No Customer Visits"**

1. In the same **"Edit profile"** section
2. Look for **"Business information"** or **"Attributes"**
3. Find the section about **"Customer visits"** or **"Visit options"**
4. Look for an option that says:
   - **"No customer visits"** or
   - **"Customers don't visit this location"** or
   - **"Service-area business - no storefront"**
5. **Enable/Check this option**

---

### **Alternative Method: Through Business Information**

If you can't find the options above, try this:

1. Click **"Edit profile"**
2. Go to **"Business information"** tab
3. Look for **"Business attributes"** or **"Attributes"**
4. Scroll through the attributes list
5. Find and enable:
   - ‚úÖ **"Service-area business"**
   - ‚úÖ **"No customer visits"** or **"No storefront"**

---

### **Step 4: Add Your Service Areas**

Once you've enabled service-area business:

1. In the **"Service areas"** section
2. Click **"Add service area"** or **"Add location"**
3. Start typing city names and select from dropdown:
   - **Delaware:** Dover, DE
   - **Texas:** Houston, TX; Dallas, TX; Austin, TX; San Antonio, TX; Fort Worth, TX
   - **Florida:** Miami, FL; Orlando, FL; Tampa, FL; Jacksonville, FL; Fort Lauderdale, FL
   - **California:** Los Angeles, CA; San Diego, CA; Sacramento, CA; San Jose, CA; Fresno, CA
   - **Georgia:** Atlanta, GA; Savannah, GA; Augusta, GA; Macon, GA; Columbus, GA
   - **Illinois:** Chicago, IL; Springfield, IL; Peoria, IL; Rockford, IL; Naperville, IL
4. Add each city one by one (all 25 cities)
5. Click **"Save"** when done

---

### **Step 5: Verify Your Settings**

After saving, verify that:

- ‚úÖ **"Service-area business"** is enabled
- ‚úÖ **"No customer visits"** is enabled
- ‚úÖ All 25 service areas are listed
- ‚úÖ Your business address (8 The Green STE R, Dover, DE 19901) is still listed (for verification only)

---

## üéØ **What This Does**

### **Service-Area Business:**
- Tells Google you deliver to customers rather than having them visit you
- Helps Google understand your business model
- Improves local SEO for service-area searches

### **No Customer Visits:**
- Indicates you don't have a physical storefront
- Prevents customers from trying to visit your address
- Ensures Google knows the Dover address is for verification only

---

## ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important Notes**

1. **Address Still Required:** Even as a service-area business, you need to keep your Dover, DE address for verification purposes. Google will understand it's not a customer-facing location.

2. **Service Areas vs. Address:** 
   - Your **address** (Dover, DE) = Verification/legal address
   - Your **service areas** = Where you actually serve customers (25 cities)

3. **Visibility:** Once verified and these attributes are set, your business will appear in local searches for all 25 cities you serve.

4. **Can't Find the Options?** 
   - Make sure your business is verified first (some options only appear after verification)
   - Try accessing from desktop browser (mobile app may have limited options)
   - Contact Google Business Profile support if options are missing

## üÜò **Troubleshooting**

### **Can't find "Service-area business" option?**

1. **Check if verified:** Some options only appear after verification
2. **Try different section:** Look in "Business information" ‚Üí "Attributes"
3. **Desktop vs Mobile:** Use desktop browser for full options
4. **Category matters:** Make sure category is "Portable toilet supplier"

### **Can't add service areas?**

1. **Enable service-area business first:** This unlocks the service areas section
2. **Use city, state format:** Type "Houston, TX" not just "Houston"
3. **One at a time:** Add cities individually, not all at once

### **Options grayed out?**

- Your business may need to be verified first
- Some attributes require verification before they can be changed
- Contact Google support if options remain unavailable
